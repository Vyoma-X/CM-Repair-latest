Refund And Cancellation Policy

Home> Shop> Refund And Cancellation Policy

Last updated: April 2025

At Pro Mobile Aberdeen, we stand by the quality of our repairs and services. This policy outlines our approach to refunds, returns, and repair warranties.

1. Repair Guarantee & Warranty

We offer a 6-month warranty on all qualifying repairs carried out by our technicians.

What's Covered:

  • Defects directly caused by our repair work (e.g., faulty parts fitted by us or workmanship issues).

  • Hardware faults that reoccur due to the original repair issue.

What's Not Covered:

  • Water or liquid damage (even if it occurs after the repair).

  • New faults unrelated to the original repair.

  • Physical damage (cracked screens, bent frames, etc.) after the repair.

  • Damage caused by misuse, third-party repair attempts, or tampering.

2. Warranty Conditions

  • Warranty is valid only for the original customer and applies only to the device originally repaired.

  • Devices must be returned in the same condition as received (aside from the repair)

  • Proof of original repair (receipt or reference) is required for all warranty claims.

3. Faulty Repairs – Our Responsibility

If your device shows signs of the same issue after repair and it's determined the fault is due to our parts or workmanship, we will:

  • Re-assess and re-repair the device at no extra cost, or

  • Offer a full or partial refund, depending on the situation and if repair is not possible.

4. Refunds for Repairs

We do not offer refunds for completed repairs unless:

  • The issue remains unresolved due to our fault, and

  • We are unable to repair the issue after re-evaluation.

  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–10 business days.

5. How to Claim a Warranty or Refund

To make a claim, contact us by email or visit the store with:

  • Your device

  • Proof of repair (receipt or repair reference)

  • A clear description of the fault

  • We will assess the issue and advise you on the next steps.
